SLCC to University of Utah

Chemical Engineering Transfer Pathway

Ensure your completed Salt Lake Community College courses align perfectly with the U's graduation track to join us as a full junior in the Fall.

⚠️ Engineering Prerequisite Sequencing Note: Chemical engineering coursework is heavily sequential. Missing critical sophomore classes like CHE 2800 or ENGR 2300 before transferring means you will be missing prerequisites for the junior-level design sequence, which can delay your graduation timeline by a full academic year.
  • All courses listed below must be completed with a letter grade of "C" or higher to transfer successfully.
