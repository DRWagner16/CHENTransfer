Standard U of U Lower-Division Curriculum Plan

This timeline outlines how native students progress through lower-division requirements. Transfer paths can use the optional summer to break up heavy course loads.

Semester 1 (Fall)

  • CH EN 1703 - Intro to Chem. Eng.
  • CH EN 2953 - Career & Prof Dev
  • MATH 1210 - Calculus I
  • CHEM 1210 - General Chemistry I
  • CHEM 1215 - General Chemistry I Lab
  • WRTG 1010 - Intro to Writing

Semester 2 (Spring)

  • CH EN 1705 - ChE Design & Innovation
  • MATH 1220 - Calculus II
  • CHEM 1220 - General Chemistry II
  • CHEM 1225 - General Chemistry II Lab
  • PHYS 2210 - Physics for Sci & Eng I
  • WRTG 2010 - Intermediate Writing

Optional Summer Term

  • MATH 2210 - Calculus III
  • General Education / Electives
  • Catch-up Math if required
  • Pre-major foundational labs

Semester 3 (Fall)

  • CH EN 2300 - Thermodynamics I
  • CH EN 2450 - Numerical Methods
  • MATH 2250 - Diff Eq & Linear Algebra
  • PHYS 2220 - Physics for Sci & Eng II

Semester 4 (Spring)

  • CH EN 2800 - Process Engineering Fund.
  • CH EN 2550 - Stats for Chemical Eng.
  • CH EN 2955 - Undergrad Seminar (suggested)
  • CHEM 2310 - Organic Chemistry I
