SLCC to University of Utah

Chemical Engineering Transfer Pathway Portal

Ensure your completed Salt Lake Community College courses directly sync with the U's graduation timeline to hit the ground running as a full junior in the Fall semester.

⚠️ Core Major Progression Bottleneck Alert: To register for upper-division classes at the U (such as CH EN 3353: Fluid Mechanics and CH EN 3453: Heat Transfer) in your first Fall semester, you must satisfy specific technical requirements.
  • All courses must be passed with a grade of "C" or better.
  • Failing to complete sophomore engineering classes (like material balances) prior to transferring will push your graduation date back by a full academic calendar year.
